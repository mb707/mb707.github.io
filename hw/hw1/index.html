Overview

Artificial intelligence agents are software systems that can perform tasks with a specific goal in mind. In software development, these agents may help write code, review errors, explain documentation, create tests, or organize a project into smaller steps.

Instead of replacing programmers, AI agents are most useful when they act as assistants. A programmer still needs to understand the problem, check the output, test the code, and make final decisions.
